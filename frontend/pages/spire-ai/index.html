AI-Powered Creation

Use ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI to generate quiz questions, export as CSV, and import them here.

Example prompt: "Generate 20 quiz questions about [topic] in CSV format with columns: question, correct_answer, wrong_answer_1, wrong_answer_2, wrong_answer_3, explanation, difficulty"

CSV Format

ColumnReqInfo
question✅The question text
correct_answer✅The correct answer
wrong_answer_1✅First wrong answer
wrong_answer_2-Second wrong answer
wrong_answer_3-Third wrong answer
explanation-Shown after answering
difficulty-easy / medium / hard / expert

Paste Raw Text

Paste AI-generated quiz content as raw text and we'll parse it into questions automatically.

How It Works

Paste text from ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI in a simple format. Each question block should follow this pattern:

Q: What is the capital of France?
A: Paris (correct)
A: London
A: Berlin
A: Madrid
E: Paris has been the capital since the 10th century
D: easy

Q: What is 2+2?
A: 4 (correct)
A: 3
A: 5
E: Basic arithmetic
D: easy

Mark correct answers with (correct). Lines starting with E: are explanations, D: is difficulty (easy/medium/hard/expert).
