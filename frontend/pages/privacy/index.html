Privacy Policy

Welcome to Quiz The Spire! We value your privacy and transparency regarding how we collect, use, and share data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our quiz game, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

What information do we collect?

We collect various types of information to enhance your gaming experience and optimize the functionality of Quiz The Spire:

  • Game Data: Information about your quiz performance, points scored, items used, and game progress is collected to improve the game and display your results.
  • Technical Data: Data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type may be automatically collected for diagnostic purposes and to ensure the stability of our service.

How do we use your information?

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • To personalize and improve gameplay and develop new features.
  • For internal analysis to understand game performance and develop new features.
  • To ensure the stability and security of our service.

Sharing information with third parties

We do not share your information with third parties, except as described below:

In this case, Google, as a third party, may place and read cookies in your browser, and use web beacons or IP addresses to collect information through the display of ads on our website. You can find more information about how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps via this link: How Google uses data.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if legally required, or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights or property, or protect the safety of our users or the public.

Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to correct it, or to delete it. Please contact us at our support chat to exercise your rights.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
