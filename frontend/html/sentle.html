How to Play Sentle

A unique twist on Wordle! Guess each word of the daily sentence in 5 tries, then arrange them in order!

Stage 1: Word-by-Word Guessing

  • You'll see one word at a time
  • You have 5 attempts to guess each word (just like Wordle!)
  • After each guess, letters change color:
    • 🟩 Green: Letter in correct position
    • 🟨 Yellow: Letter in word but wrong position
    • ⬜ Gray: Letter not in word
  • Guess all words to move to the next stage

Stage 2: Arrange the Sentence

  • You'll see all the guessed words scrambled
  • Drag and drop them into the correct order
  • Submit to complete the game!

Scoring:

  • Base: 500 points
  • Bonus: 100 points per unused attempt across all words
  • Perfect game (all 1st tries): 1000+ points!

A new sentence is available each day at midnight!
