Frequently Asked Questions (Updated)

Why did my download fail after 10 attempts?

We now limit retries to 10 attempts to give you faster feedback when there's a problem. This is better than waiting 15+ minutes with hundreds of failed attempts. If a download fails, it usually means:

  • The video is restricted (age, region, or private)
  • YouTube is temporarily blocking the request
  • The video URL is invalid or the video was deleted

Solution: Try again in a few minutes, or use the cookie upload feature for restricted content.

How do I download age-restricted videos?

Use the cookie upload feature:

  1. Install the "Get cookies.txt" browser extension
  2. Go to YouTube.com while logged in
  3. Export cookies using the extension
  4. Upload the cookies.txt file using our cookie manager
  5. Your downloads will now use your YouTube session
How many videos can I download at once?

You can have up to 5 active downloads at the same time. This includes both single videos and playlists. Additional requests will be queued automatically.

Our system can process 12 videos simultaneously across all users, so popular times may experience slight delays.
