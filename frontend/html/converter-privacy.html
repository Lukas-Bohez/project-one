Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

As the developer of this Chrome extension, I am committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

1. Who We Are

This privacy policy applies to the Chrome extension developed by Oroka Conner ("we," "our," or "I"). We believe in building tools that respect user privacy and data rights. Your trust is important to us.

📊 What Data We Collect

🎯 Data You Provide

  • User Preferences: Settings and preferences you configure
  • Content URLs: URLs you choose to process (temporarily)

📈 Automatically Collected

  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage data (optional)
  • Error Reports: Crash reports to improve stability
  • Browser Info: Basic browser/OS info (anonymized)

🔧 How We Use Your Data

We use data responsibly and only for these specific purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide the extension's features
  • Improvement: Anonymous analytics to enhance the extension
  • Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide help
  • Security: To detect and prevent abuse

🔐 Data Storage & Security

We prioritize local storage and minimal data retention:

  • Local Storage: Most data stays on your device
  • Temporary Processing: URLs processed temporarily and cleared
  • Automatic Cleanup: Temporary data automatically removed
  • No Servers: Data stays within your browser

🤝 Third-Party Services

YouTube API

For video processing functionality (subject to YouTube's privacy policy)

Analytics (Optional)

Anonymous usage tracking to improve the extension (can be disabled)

🎛️ Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your privacy:

  • Disable Tracking: Turn off analytics in extension settings
  • Clear Data: Reset extension data through Chrome
  • Uninstall: Remove extension and all data instantly
  • Contact Us: Reach out with privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension or our website. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
