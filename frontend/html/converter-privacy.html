Privacy Policy

Last Updated: october 25, 2025

🔒 Your Privacy is Our Priority

Convert The Spire is committed to protecting your privacy. We don't store your videos, sell your data, or track your activity. This policy explains exactly how we handle your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Convert The Spire ("we," "our," or "us"). We provide a free video conversion service focused on YouTube playlist conversions. This Privacy Policy explains how we transiently process video URLs and converted files, our automatic deletion policies, and how we protect your privacy. Support for additional platforms is planned (Coming soon).

Owner-only notice: This Service is intended to be used only to convert videos you own or have explicit permission to use. We do not condone using the Service to download copyrighted content without authorization.

2. Information We Collect

🎯 What We Actually Collect

We believe in radical transparency. Here's exactly what data we handle:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Video URLs: We temporarily process the video URLs you provide for conversion purposes only. These are NOT stored permanently.
  • Nothing Else: We don't require registration, personal details, or payment information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Basic Usage Data: We may collect anonymous statistics about conversion frequency and popular platforms (no personal identification).
  • Technical Information: Basic device information like browser type and operating system for compatibility and security.
  • IP Addresses: Temporarily logged for security and abuse prevention (automatically deleted within 24 hours).
  • Essential Cookies Only: We use minimal cookies for functionality - no tracking or advertising cookies.

3. What We DON'T Do

🚫 Zero Tolerance for Privacy Invasion

  • ❌ We DON'T store your converted videos or audio files
  • ❌ We DON'T sell, share, or monetize your data
  • ❌ We DON'T use tracking cookies or analytics that identify you
  • ❌ We DON'T require accounts or personal information
  • ❌ We DON'T assist or facilitate copyright infringement — you must have rights or permission to any content you convert
  • ❌ We DON'T share data with advertisers or third parties

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the minimal information we collect for these purposes only:

  • Service Provision: To process video conversions and deliver converted files to you.
  • Security & Abuse Prevention: To detect and prevent malicious activity or service abuse.
  • Communication: To respond to your support requests and inquiries.
  • Service Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve our service (anonymized data only).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws when legally required.

5. Data Retention and Automatic Deletion

⏱️ Automatic Data Cleanup

We believe in "privacy by design" - your data is automatically deleted:

  • Video URLs: Processed temporarily and deleted within 10 minutes of conversion completion
  • Converted Files: Automatically deleted from our servers within 2 hours
  • Server Logs: Technical logs are automatically purged every 24 hours
  • Download Links: Expire and become inaccessible after download or 2 hours (whichever comes first)

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We have a strict no-sharing policy with the following limited exceptions:

  • Essential Service Providers: Cloud hosting providers who help us deliver the service (they cannot access your personal data)
  • Legal Requirements: Only when required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • Safety & Security: To prevent fraud, abuse, or protect user safety (anonymized data only)
  • Never for Marketing: We will NEVER share your data for advertising, marketing, or commercial purposes

7. International Data Transfers

Our service may process data on servers in different countries to provide optimal performance. We ensure that any international transfers include appropriate safeguards to protect your privacy, regardless of location.

8. Your Rights and Control

🎛️ You're in Complete Control

Your privacy rights are important to us. Here's what you can do:

  • Access: Request information about any data we may have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any data (though most is auto-deleted anyway)
  • Correction: Correct any inaccurate information
  • Portability: Get a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to data processing (you can always stop using our service)

  • Right to Complain: You can lodge complaints with your local data protection authority
  • Data Protection Officer: Contact our support chat for GDPR-related inquiries
