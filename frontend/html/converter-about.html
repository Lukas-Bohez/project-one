❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Convert The Spire completely free?

We believe that basic internet tools should be accessible to everyone. Convert The Spire is our contribution to making the web a better place. The service is sustainable through efficient design and community support.

How do you make money if the service is free?

Convert The Spire is currently a passion project. The costs are minimal due to efficient architecture, and we maintain it as a service to the community. There are no premium tiers, and no hidden monetization.

Is my data safe with Convert The Spire?

Absolutely. We don't store your videos, track your activity, or collect personal information. All processing is temporary, and files are automatically deleted within hours. Your privacy is our top priority.

Will Convert The Spire always be free?

Yes, that's a core commitment. Convert The Spire will always remain free to use. This will never change, regardless of how the service grows.
