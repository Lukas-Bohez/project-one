🚀 How to Use Public Domain Works

For Creators & Artists

  • Remix classic literature into modern stories
  • Create new music using public domain melodies
  • Design products featuring famous artworks
  • Make films inspired by classic plots
  • Develop educational content without licensing costs

For Educators & Students

  • Access complete works for literature classes
  • Study art history with unlimited image reproduction
  • Analyze films without copyright restrictions
  • Create derivative works for assignments
  • Build digital libraries for institutions

For Businesses

  • Use classic designs in marketing materials
  • Create merchandise featuring public domain characters
  • Develop apps and games with classic themes
  • Produce educational content at low cost
  • Build content libraries for subscription services

💡 Pro Tip: Always verify copyright status before commercial use. While public domain works are free, some adaptations or new works may have their own copyright protection.
