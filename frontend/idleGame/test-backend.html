Game Instructions

How to use Industrial Empire with backend save system:

  1. First, make sure the backend server is running on port 8000
  2. Click "Login" in the game to create an account or sign in
  3. Your game progress will be automatically saved every 30 seconds
  4. Use "Save Game" to manually save your progress
  5. Use "Load Game" to restore your saved progress
  6. Your progress is tied to your account and will persist across sessions
